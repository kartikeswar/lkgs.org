For parents and families

A gentle home for LKGS parents who need clarity, courage, and community.

This website is designed as a warm, trustworthy blog for families navigating cleft lip and palate, also called Lippen-Kiefer-Gaumenspalte (LKGS). It gives space for honest stories, practical help, and links to trusted organizations including Deutsche Cleft Kinderhilfe e.V.

What this site can do
Support families emotionally.

Use calm language, relatable articles, and hopeful stories that reduce isolation.

Share practical guidance.

Add feeding advice, surgery preparation notes, speech therapy journeys, and hospital checklists.

Direct people to trusted help.

Feature nonprofit links, parent communities, and donation pathways without overwhelming visitors.

Mission

Parents deserve support that feels human.

The first weeks after diagnosis or birth can feel heavy. This site is structured to meet parents with calm words, clear next steps, and a sense that they are not facing this alone.

Voice

Warm, practical, and stigma-free.

The writing style avoids medical coldness and avoids outdated, hurtful terms. It respects both the child and the family while still sharing helpful details.

Growth
